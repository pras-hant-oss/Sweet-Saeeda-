Our Story

Taste the Soul
of Tunisia

Located in the historic Leeds Kirkgate Market, Sweet Saeeda is a beloved counter-serve nook that brings the vibrant flavors of Tunisia to Yorkshire. Every dish is crafted with passion, using fresh ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

From our signature Makloub bread to the rich, aromatic Tunisian Ojja, each bite tells a story of tradition, warmth, and culinary excellence. Whether you are grabbing a quick lunch or settling in for a leisurely meal, we promise an unforgettable experience.
